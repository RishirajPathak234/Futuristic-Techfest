About Quantum Horizon 2025

Ramdeobaba Institute's original Technical Fest is finally here. Excited?

🔬 Where Innovation Meets the Future! Get ready to dive into the future of technology at Quantum Horizon 2025—a one-of-a-kind futuristic techfest bringing together innovators, tech enthusiasts, and industry leaders! Scheduled for April 1, 2025, this event promises to be a groundbreaking fusion of AI, Quantum Computing, Robotics, Space Tech, and more.

🌟 What to Expect?

✅ Cutting-Edge Workshops – Explore Quantum Computing, AI-driven automation, and Next-Gen Cybersecurity.

✅ Tech Challenges & Hackathons – Solve futuristic problems and showcase your coding brilliance!

✅ Expert Talks & Panel Discussions – Gain insights from pioneers in AI, Blockchain, and SpaceTech.

✅ Startup & Innovation Expo – Witness the next big thing in deep tech!

✅ Virtual Reality & Metaverse Zones – Step into the future with immersive experiences.

🔹 Whether you’re a tech geek, a coding wizard, or an innovator, Quantum Horizon 2025 is the place to be! Let’s shape the future together.

1st April 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Grand Opening Ceremony & AI & Quantum Computing Summit.

By the Hands of Shri. Banwarilal Purohitji

The Quantum Horizon 2025 techfest will begin with a grand opening ceremony, welcoming renowned scientists, industry leaders, and tech enthusiasts from around the world. The day will kick off with keynote speeches from pioneers in AI, Quantum Computing, and Futuristic Technologies, setting the stage for a thrilling event.

Location: DT-Main Auditorium, Ramdeobaba College

2nd April 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

RoboWars

Day 2 will be all about automation, cybersecurity, and robotics. The morning will start with the much-anticipated RoboWars (Elimination Rounds), where autonomous battle robots will compete in thrilling duels. This event will test the strength, agility, and strategy of each robotic warrior. Simultaneously, the Humanoid Challenge will showcase advanced humanoid robots capable of performing complex tasks, highlighting the progress in AI-driven robotics.

Location: DT-Main Auditorium, Ramdeobaba College

3rd April 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Future of SpaceTech Talk.

Day 3 will take attendees beyond Earth, exploring the frontiers of space technology and futuristic innovations. The morning will kick off with the Future of SpaceTech Talk, featuring scientists, engineers, and astronauts discussing interplanetary exploration, AI-driven space missions, and how technology is making Mars colonization possible.

Location: DT-08 Floor Room No. 810, Ramdeobaba College.

3rd April 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Final Round – Quantum Code Sprint Hackathon

The Quantum Code Sprint Hackathon (Final Round) will see the top 10 teams present their final projects, demonstrating AI, Blockchain, and Quantum innovations. Simultaneously, the AI Bot Battle Final Round will feature the most advanced AI-powered bots competing against one another for the ultimate title.

Location: CSE Building Near Aeroplane, Ramdeobaba College.

4th April 09:00 PM 12:00 Noon

RoboWars Grand Finale

For Hack Busters

The morning will begin with the RoboWars Grand Finale, where the two strongest robots will engage in an epic last battle. The arena will be filled with excitement as autonomous and manually controlled bots clash in a test of strategy, durability, and power. Meanwhile, the Drone Racing Finals will take place, where the best FPV drone pilots will push their skills to the limit in a high-speed obstacle course.

Location: OAT, Ramdeobaba College.

12:00 Noon - 3:00 PM

Drone Racing Finals/

For Electronics Guys

Rules:

  1. Maximum drone weight: 2 kg.
  2. Only FPV drones are allowed.
  3. Racing time limit: 5 minutes per round.
  4. Controllers allowed: Arduino, Raspberry Pi.
  5. Crashed drones will be disqualified unless fixed within 2 minutes.

Location: ENCS Dept Ground, Ramdeobaba College.

6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Closing Ceremony & Award Distribution

As the festival draws to a close, the Closing Ceremony & Award Distribution will take center stage. Winners from all major competitions, including the Quantum Code Sprint Hackathon, Cyber Warfare CTF, RoboWars, AI Bot Battle, and Drone Racing, will be honored with trophies, cash prizes, internships, and exclusive opportunities with leading tech firms. Special awards will also be given to the most innovative project, best startup idea, and most promising young innovator.

Location: Main Audi, Ramdeobaba College.
